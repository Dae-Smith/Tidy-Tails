About Tidy Tails

Born in Houston. Tailored for busy pet parents.

Tidy Tails was founded in 2025 by three lifelong friends who shared a love for dogs — and a distaste for cleaning up after them. With backgrounds in IT, logistics, and healthcare, Dae Smith, Aury Driver, and Ashe Milligan combined their skills to solve a common problem for pet owners: dog poop.

We provide professional dog waste removal services in Katy, Cypress, and East Houston. We’re on a mission to give pet families cleaner yards, more free time, and a stink-free life — one scoop at a time.

From our eco-conscious disposal methods to our friendly scoopers who actually love their job, everything we do is centered around one thing: keeping tails tidy and customers happy.
